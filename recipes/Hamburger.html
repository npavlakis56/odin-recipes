Hamburger

Image: A delicious looking picture of our award winning Hamburger

This is a simple and easy recipe that only takes a few minutes to make!

Learn how to make this delicious dish by using the below ingredients:

  • 3 Hamburger Buns
  • 1lb of lean ground beef
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Cheese *optional*

Here are the steps you will need to complete this reciepe:

  1. Turn grill or heating surface on, heat to 400F
  2. Form ground beef into 3 patties
  3. Season the patties with salt and pepper
  4. Add patties to grill, cook for 5 minutes each side
  5. While patties grill, chop up the lettuce and tomato
  6. *Optional* Add cheese to patties 1 minute before final side is done
  7. Take patties of grill on put on open buns
  8. Add Ketchup and Mustard to top bun
  9. Add Lettuce and Tomato to bun
  10. Enjoy!
