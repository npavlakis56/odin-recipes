Cookies

Image: A delicious looking picture of our award winning Cookies

This is a simple and easy recipe that only takes a few minutes to make!

Learn how to make this delicious dish by using the below ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate Chips

Here are the steps you will need to complete this reciepe:

  1. Heat oven to 450F
  2. Mix all ingredients together
  3. Portion dough into cookie size pieces
  4. Place pieces onto baking tray
  5. Set tray in oven and bake for 20 min
  6. Take out of oven and enjoy!
