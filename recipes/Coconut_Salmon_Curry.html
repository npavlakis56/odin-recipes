Coconut Salmon Curry

Image: A healthy portion of our award winning Coconut Salmon Curry

This is a simple and easy recipe that only takes a between 20-30 minutes to make!

Learn how to make this delicious dish by using the below ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Coconut
  • Curry

Here are the steps you will need to complete this reciepe:

  1. Season the Salmon
  2. Heat up pan on medium heat and add Curry, let cook for 2 minutes
  3. Add Coconut to pot and bring to a boil, then simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Add Salmon and cook for 8 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy
